About DoseDash
Medical Supplies & Medication Tracker

Purpose

DoseDash is designed to be a comprehensive solution for caregivers in elderly care facilities. It provides tools for tracking medications, managing supplies, and ensuring that residents receive their prescribed treatments on schedule.

Core Features

Resident Management
Maintain profiles for each resident with their medication needs and schedules
Inventory Tracking
Keep track of medication quantities, expiration dates, and usage patterns
Usage Logging
Record when medications are taken or missed, with optional voice notes
Alerts & Notifications
Receive timely alerts for low stock, missed doses, or upcoming expirations
Reporting
Generate comprehensive reports on medication usage and inventory status

Caregiver Workflow

Login and access your personalized dashboard
Select a resident to view their medication schedule
Review any notifications for low-stock or missed doses
Log doses as Taken or Missed, optionally with voice notes
Manage inventory levels and generate reports as needed
Getting Started

If you're a caregiver or administrator, login to access the system. If you need an account, please contact your facility administrator.
